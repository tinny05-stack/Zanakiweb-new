Admission Process

Join our community of learners and achievers

Step-by-Step Admission Process

1

Application Submission

Complete and submit the admission application form along with required documents. Applications can be submitted online or in person at our admissions office.

Duration: 1 day
2

Document Review

Our admissions team will review all submitted documents and verify the information provided. This process typically takes 3-5 business days.

Duration: 3-5 days
3

Assessment Test

Students will take an age-appropriate assessment test to evaluate their current academic level and learning needs.

Duration: 2 hours
4

Interview

A brief interview with the student and parents/guardians to discuss expectations, goals, and school policies.

Duration: 30 minutes
5

Admission Decision

Admission decision will be communicated within one week of completing all requirements. Successful applicants will receive enrollment instructions.

Duration: 1 week
6

Enrollment

Complete the enrollment process by paying fees, submitting additional forms, and attending the orientation session.

Duration: 1 day
